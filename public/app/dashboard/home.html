Air Bears

Mission
To utilize UAS "drone" technology for the benefit of all life.
How
By creating & maintaining a global network of volunteer RC pilots dedicated to aiding local authorities in any capacity.

Services

Whether it's personnel training or acclimating your equestrian and K-9 divisions to drones, Air Bears provides the knowledge, equipment, and expertise needed to effectively integrate UAS into your organization.

Air Bears is dedicated to locating missing or lost loved ones and reuniting them with their families.

Firefighting/suppression, criminal apprehension, or accident investigation. Whatever the case may be, if having a bird's eye view of the situation may help, Air Bears are here to serve.
